Smart Hood App

Project Goal

Make an app for smart hood. The app can not only control the hood but also monitor the status of the filter.

Binding process

To connect the hood, you have to find it in the Wi-Fi list of your mobile, then connect it to your WI-Fi.

Homepage

In the homepaege, you can change the fan speed, light, and timer of the hood.

My Hood

In this page, you can check on the status of filters and buy them by clicking the shopping cart.

User page

You can manage your account and monitor your hood's status in this page.
